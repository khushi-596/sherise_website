Who We Are

About She Can Foundation

A structured team working together since 2018 to elevate underrepresented communities.

Our Visionary Story

She Can Foundation was established with a singular focus: to offer actionable resources, community learning frameworks, and structured mentorship programs to women seeking absolute self-reliance.

We work closely with community leaders to identify specific blocks where women are kept out of basic employment due to lack of educational resources or tech skills. By setting up physical vocational hubs and offering tablet learning kits, we enable students to learn sewing, accounting, or fundamental computer skills at their own pace.

Since our inception in Mumbai, we have empowered over 1200 women through certifications, leading to small business setups or office jobs.

Pillars of Operation

Our Mission

To deliver certified training courses, financial scholarships, and mentor matches that break barriers and open independent paths.

Milestones

Timeline of Impact

A chronological record of our foundation milestones and expansion.

2018

Foundation Started

Opened She Can Foundation in Mumbai, starting with an active force of 10 volunteer mentors guiding 20 adult sewing students.

2020

Tablet learning Program

Adapted to virtual teaching, distributing basic learning tablets and supporting 300+ students through remote mentorship tracks.

2022

Central vocational hub

Inaugurated a dedicated vocational skill training hub with active computer classrooms and handicraft design labs in Mumbai.

2024

1200+ Graduates Milestone

Celebrated certifiying 1,200+ female graduates from all our computer literacy and sewing courses, supported by 500+ volunteers.
