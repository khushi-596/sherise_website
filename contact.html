Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Find immediate answers regarding volunteering, program enrollments, and support.

Who can enroll in She Can Foundation programs?

All programs are completely free and open to young girls, women, and mothers seeking skill development, computer literacy, or academic scholarship support. Simply reach out or visit our center.

What is the duration of the vocational and computer courses?

Our core vocational and digital technology skills courses run for 12 weeks, with lessons twice a week. We also hold individual weekend workshops on leadership and legal rights.

How can I apply to become a volunteer mentor?

You can fill out our application form directly on the "Volunteer" page. Our volunteer coordinator reviews forms weekly and schedules orientation sessions once candidates are matched.

Are the programs certified?

Yes, all graduates of our computer literacy, sewing, and leadership courses receive a signed foundation certificate verifying their course completion hours and newly acquired skills.

Where do She Can Foundation donations go?

Over 85% of all received funds directly go to procuring computers, study books, vocational sewing supplies, and funding scholarship grants. We maintain complete financial records.
